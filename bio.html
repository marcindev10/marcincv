Marcin Selecki

English Instructor, Front-End Developer (Tekirdag, Turkey)

I'm an experienced, energetic and up-to-date English Teacher, helping a wide range of people (0-99 years old) become fluent in English. The most important value I have as a teacher is 'passion' and 'love' for the people I teach especially children and my profession -which also means dedication, long hours, studying, following the latest approaches -and this happens because teaching is not only a job for a living it is also living for the job.

When I’m not on the job, I love jogging, playing tennis, rollerblading and bird watching. I like to work my way through every recipe in my cookbook, and satiate my passion for seeing new places.

Ever since I was a young boy I've had two passions in life; birds and languages. Today as an English Teacher of 12 years, I still love learning and playing with languages, being creative with words and passing this over to other people because I think imagination is more important than knowledge.

As for the birds, I go out to the nature regularly and catch some amazing shots while enjoying their company.

Now for the future, I am really into coding and digital world , enhancing my skills with online courses and practices in the field. If you believe that I can be of help to you, please contact me.
